Game sample: Ping pong

How to:
Use the arrow keys up and down to move, or [W] and [S].
Press spacebar or enter, to start a next round, or to pause/resume.

To restart the game use the buttons below. Or open the terminal, type "restartpong" and press enter.

To get more information about pong open the terminal, type "printpong" and press enter.

When DEBUG_PONG_PARENT_GLOBAL is true, open the terminal, type "popvar game" and press enter.
